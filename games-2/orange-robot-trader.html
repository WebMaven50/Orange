How the market works

Daily & Weekly candles are 100% real. Every 1D and 1W candle is pulled directly from actual historical Bitcoin prices (2010–2024). Nothing is invented at this resolution.

15m / 1H / 4H candles are simulated within real bounds. Minute-level historical data isn't available here, so intraday movement is procedurally generated — but it's mathematically constrained to start/end at that day's real open and close, and never exceed that day's real high/low.

Trading: A 0.26% fee applies to every order, like a real exchange. Selling more BTC than you hold opens a short position — powerful, but it's how you can go bankrupt if the price moves against you.

Sleep: Passes 8 game-hours in 3 real seconds. Price keeps moving while you're out — you just can't trade during it.

Cash Out: You don't have to wait for $1M or bankruptcy — hit Cash Out anytime to lock in your current equity as your final score and save it to the leaderboard.

Order types: Market fills instantly at the current price. Limit orders fill once the price reaches your chosen level (at that limit price). Stop orders trigger once price crosses your level, then fill at the next market price — like a real exchange, a fast-moving price can mean your fill isn't exactly at the stop price. Trailing stops follow the best price by your chosen dollar distance and fill at market once price pulls back that far. There's no real order book here, so no partial fills or slippage beyond that — every fill is treated as fully executed at the price described above.
